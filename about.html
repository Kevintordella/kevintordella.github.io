Hello there!

I'm Kevin Tordella

I'm a designer based in the NJ/NYC area, born and raised in New Jersey. A big music nerd at heart, I love sharing songs with others and discovering new songs. I'm also passionate about archiving art to preserve its history. When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me at a concert, a convention, or deep down a music rabbit hole.

My career began as a self-taught video editor and graphic designer in the eSports industry. I took my skills to the next level by enrolling at Full Sail University. Soon after I realized my love for human-centered design, I knew there was still a lot to learn, so I enrolled at Rutgers University, where I'm diligently working on finishing my Master of Business and Science.
